Welcome to XspecT and ClAssT!


XspecT and ClAssT are both tools to taxonomically assign bacterial Input-Data using Bloom Filters. XspecT performs a taxonomic assignment on the Species-Level while ClAssT uses Strain-Typing to identify International-Clones of A.baumannii. Both tools are also capable to screen for existance of Oxa-families.
For more information about the tools please visit our information-page.
